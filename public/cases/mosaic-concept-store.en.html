MOSAIC — Concept Store

Art Direction & Digital Product Design

Brand / Retail 2025 — present Art Direction · UX/UI · Design System · AI
00 — Overview

MOSAIC is a multi-brand concept store currently in active development — a space at the intersection of fashion retail, digital culture and editorial aesthetics. The goal is not just an online shop, but a full digital environment with its own character, atmosphere and visual language that reads as a cultural statement.

Role Art Director & UX/UI Designer — end-to-end responsibility for visual identity, digital product and overall creative direction. Beyond web design — AI-powered campaign concepts.

01 — Scope of Work
01 Identity

Visual Identity

The brand's visual language is built from scratch — from concept to its final expression in the digital environment. The aesthetic is grounded in technological minimalism, dark interfaces, complex typography and an editorial approach — visual thinking borrowed from the worlds of fashion and contemporary media.

Every decision is driven not by trend, but by the brand's internal logic: wordmark, typographic scale, composition rules, photographic language, campaign tone and the principles of working with space on screen and in print.

02 Product

Digital Product Design

Full product design for the site: from information architecture and UX scenarios to the final visual layer. The key user paths are designed end-to-end — landing, catalogue with filters and sorting, product card, cart and checkout flow.

A working interactive prototype is assembled in Figma with transitions, states and micro-interactions — for usability testing and lossless handoff to engineering. The responsive system is built for desktop, tablet and mobile breakpoints.

03 System

Design System

A full Figma design system built on Variables: colour tokens (primitive + semantic), typographic scale, spacing grid, radius and elevation tokens. Theming (dark / light / contrast) is implemented via mode switching of the variables.

The component library is built on Auto Layout with a complete set of States and Variants: buttons, inputs, product cards, navigation, dialogs, sheets, snackbars. The system is designed to scale with the product and hold consistency across every screen and scenario.

03 — Design Philosophy

MOSAIC is designed as a space where every element carries meaning. The interface is not a neutral shell for products, but part of the brand's communication.

Typography works as an editorial instrument. The dark colour system builds atmosphere. Motion and blur are not decoration but part of the product's character. The goal is to create a digital product that the user feels — not just uses.
